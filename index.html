About TJ's Snacks

Manufacturing high quality snacks since 1998.

TJ's Snacks is a snack manufacturing company for the wholesale market, based in Florida, Johannesburg. Our diverse product range includes Naks, Puffs, Puffed Corn and our famous Mielie Pops.

We strive to provide our customers with the highest quality product, with excellent service and extremely competitive pricing. Our products are sold throughout most provinces in South Africa and we have established an export market to some of our neighbouring countries.

Our strive for excellence and our commitment to honest business practice is what has been the cornerstone into TJ's Snacks growing into the company it has become today.
