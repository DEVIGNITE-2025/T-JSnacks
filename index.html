About TJ Snacks

A proudly established manufacturer with a premium growth mindset.

TJ Snacks is a snack manufacturing business for the wholesale market, based in Florida, Johannesburg. Since 1998, the business has built a reputation for quality products, competitive pricing and dependable service.

The range includes Naks, Puffs, Puffed Corn and Mielie Pops, sold across multiple provinces and into neighbouring export markets, with new flavour categories and sauces now being introduced.
